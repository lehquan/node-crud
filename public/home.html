Fast money transfer to any Crypto Exchange

Sint is the first platform providing instant exchange transactions: Cryptocurrency to FIAT and FIAT to cryptocurrency.

THE IDEA

Immediate conversion of assets

It is based fully on the blockchain technology. It was created by a team of experienced developers and managers. It allows a fluent transfer of assets, in form of FIFAT currency or any cryptocurrency, from any cryptocurrency exchange in the world to your account.

The way the platform works can be described in 4 easy steps

When you need to fund a wallet with cryptocurrency

  1. Choose the cryptocurrency you want to receive.
  2. Specify the address of a wallet where you want to receive the cryptocurrency.
  3. Deposit the FIAT currency from your credit card or a bank account with just one click using the system of instant payments.
  4. Your cryptocurrency address has been funded.

When you need to withdraw the assets, and transfer them to your credit card or bank account

  1. Choose the FIAT currency you want to receive and specify the bank account number where the money should be transferred.
  2. Choose the cryptocurrency your want to sell.
  3. You will be given a unique wallet address where you will send cryptocurrency.
  4. The money will show up on your account as soon as the confirmation issued by Blockchain network is received.
